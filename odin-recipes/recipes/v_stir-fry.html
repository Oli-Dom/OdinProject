Vegetable Stir-Fry

Instructions

  • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat.
  • Add minced garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  • Add harder vegetables first (carrots, broccoli, bell peppers), stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  • Add softer vegetables (snap peas, mushrooms, zucchini), stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Pour in soy sauce, sesame oil, and a splash of rice vinegar. Toss everything together.
  • Add a slurry of cornstarch and water to thicken the sauce if desired.
  • Stir continuously until vegetables are tender-crisp and sauce coats everything evenly.
  • Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
