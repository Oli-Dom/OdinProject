Chicken Curry

Instructions

  • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
  • Add diced onions and cook until golden, about 5 minutes.
  • Add minced garlic, ginger, and curry powder/paste. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Add diced chicken and cook until lightly browned on all sides.
  • Pour in coconut milk and chicken broth. Add diced tomatoes and salt to taste.
  • Bring to a simmer and cook for 15-20 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  • Add diced potatoes, bell peppers, and other vegetables. Cook for another 10-15 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  • Finish with a squeeze of lemon juice and fresh cilantro. Serve hot over steamed rice.
