Spaghetti Bolognese

Instructions

  • Heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  • Add diced onions, carrots, and celery. Cook for 5-7 minutes until softened.
  • Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  • Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up as it cooks, about 8-10 minutes.
  • Pour in tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes, stirring constantly.
  • Add canned crushed tomatoes, beef broth, and dried herbs (oregano, basil). Season with salt and pepper.
  • Bring to a simmer and let cook for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally. The sauce should reduce and thicken.
  • Cook spaghetti according to package directions in salted boiling water. Drain and serve topped with Bolognese sauce.
  • Garnish with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese.
